About Me

I'm a Mechatronic Systems Engineering graduate from Simon Fraser University with over 4 years of experience in embedded systems development. Currently working as Lead Embedded Systems Engineer at Sepura Home, I specialize in IoT solutions, RTOS development, and hardware-software integration for 30,000+ deployed units.

My expertise includes ESP32/STM32 microcontroller development, FreeRTOS/Zephyr programming, PCB design with Altium/KiCad, and implementing complex embedded systems for smart home automation, parking detection systems, and medical diagnostic tools. I hold the SFU MSE Leadership Distinction and have co-authored research on breast cancer detection technology.
